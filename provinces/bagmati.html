Geography & Landscape

Bagmati Province spans from the high Himalayas in the north to the Terai plains in the south, encompassing three distinct geographical regions:

  • Himalayan Region: Home to Langtang National Park and several peaks above 7,000m
  • Hilly Region: The Kathmandu Valley and surrounding mid-hills with temperate climate
  • Terai Region: Lowland plains including parts of Chitwan National Park

Cultural Diversity

Ethnic Groups

Newars

The indigenous inhabitants of Kathmandu Valley known for their rich artistic and cultural heritage.

Tamang

Tibeto-Burman ethnic group predominantly found in the northern districts with distinct Buddhist traditions.

Major Festivals

Indra Jatra

8-day festival in Kathmandu honoring Indra, the king of heaven, featuring masked dances and chariot processions.

Bisket Jatra

New Year festival in Bhaktapur featuring the tug-of-war of giant chariots.

Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

October-November (post-monsoon) and February-April (spring) offer pleasant weather and clear mountain views.

Must-Try Foods

Newari cuisine like Bara, Chatamari, Yomari, and the famous Momo dumplings.

Top Activities

Cultural tours, trekking in Langtang, wildlife safari in Chitwan, and mountain flights.
