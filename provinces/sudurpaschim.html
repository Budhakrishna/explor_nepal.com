Geography & Landscape

Sudurpashchim Province features diverse geography from the Himalayan peaks to the Terai plains:

  • Himalayan Region: Api and Saipal Himalayan ranges with peaks over 7,000m
  • Mid-Hills: Fertile valleys and forested hills with temperate climate
  • Terai Region: Lowland plains including Shuklaphanta Wildlife Reserve

Top Attractions
