Geography & Landscape

Madhesh Province occupies the southern plains of Nepal, featuring fertile agricultural land and important border crossings with India:

  • Terai Plains: Fertile agricultural land known as Nepal's "breadbasket"
  • Wildlife Reserves: Home to Parsa and Chitwan National Parks
  • Transport Hub: Contains major border crossings with India (Birgunj, Bhairahawa)

Top Attractions

Janaki Temple

Magnificent Hindu temple in Janakpur dedicated to Goddess Sita, an important pilgrimage site.

Cultural Diversity

Ethnic Groups

Madhesi

Indigenous people of the Terai plains with strong cultural ties to northern India, primarily speaking Maithili, Bhojpuri, and other languages.

Tharu

Indigenous ethnic group known for their unique culture, traditional dances, and resistance to malaria.

Major Festivals

Chhath

Important Hindu festival dedicated to the Sun God, celebrated with great fervor along riverbanks.

Holi

Festival of colors celebrated with particular enthusiasm in the Terai region.

Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

October-March when temperatures are milder (avoid April-June when it's extremely hot).

Must-Try Foods

Dal-pitha (rice flour dumplings), thekuwa (sweet snack), and traditional Tharu cuisine.

Top Activities

Jungle safari in Chitwan, pilgrimage to Janakpur, and experiencing Chhath festival.
