Nepal's Seven Provinces

koshi province

Capital: Biratnagar

Major Cities: Dharan, Itahari, Damak

Highlights: Mount Kanchenjunga, Koshi Tappu Wildlife Reserve, Baraha Chhetra

Madesh province

Capital: Janakpur

Major Cities: Birgunj, Rajbiraj, Kalaiya

Highlights: Janaki Temple, Chure Hills, Gadhimai Festival

Bagmati Province

Capital: Hetauda

Major Cities: Kathmandu, Lalitpur, Bhaktapur

Highlights: Kathmandu Valley, Chitwan National Park, Langtang National Park

Gandaki Province

Capital: Pokhara

Major Cities: Lekhnath, Gorkha, Baglung

Highlights: Annapurna Range, Phewa Lake, Manaslu Circuit

Lumbini Province

Capital: Deukhuri

Major Cities: Butwal, Siddharthanagar, Tansen

Highlights: Lumbini (Birthplace of Buddha), Tilaurakot, Bardia National Park

Karnali Province

Capital: Birendranagar

Major Cities: Surkhet, Jumla, Dailekh

Highlights: Rara Lake, Shey Phoksundo National Park, Karnali River

Sudurpashchim Province

Capital: Godawari

Major Cities: Dhangadhi, Mahendranagar, Tikapur

Highlights: Khaptad National Park, Shuklaphanta National Park, Badimalika Temple
